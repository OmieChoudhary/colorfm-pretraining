# Interview Talking Points

## What ColorFM is

A public domain-specific pretraining project for spectral reflectance and color appearance. It demonstrates the research loop at small scale: dataset design, masked pretraining, objective ablations, scaling configs, downstream evals, and failure analysis.

## Why this maps to pretraining roles

Pretraining research is not just calling a model. It is the empirical loop around data, objectives, architecture, optimization, evals, scaling, and failure modes. ColorFM gives a clean public artifact for that loop.

## How to connect to PPG

At PPG, my work touched industrial color modeling and ΔE-driven evaluation. I cannot publish proprietary data, so ColorFM recreates the same research mechanics on safe public/synthetic spectral data.

## Honest boundary

This is not frontier-scale LLM pretraining. It is a domain-scale foundation-model pretraining project designed to demonstrate the same scientific habits: controlled experiments, ablations, evals, and clear interpretation.
